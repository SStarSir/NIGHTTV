HOW TO PLAY

Night TV is a choose your own adventure following the story of a man who spends his nights watching night time tv. His life needs to change. Make the right choices to shape the future or do nothing and watch your life passing by. Many choices can lead you abruptly to an early demise, so try to avoid them by choosing wisely, but don't worry, there are checkpoins along the way.
For the best experience, we recommend to blast some Sonic Youth or Alternative TV on your speakers, and press F11 to enjoy the game in full screen.
